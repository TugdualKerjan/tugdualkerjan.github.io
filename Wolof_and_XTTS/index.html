XTTS Audio Samples

This page presents audio samples generated using three different models finetuned from XTTS (Extended Text-to-Speech) for the Wolof language:

  • BPE with large dataset: Extended BPE tokenizer (1000 additional mappings) trained on 5 unprocessed datasets
  • BPE with clean dataset: Extended BPE tokenizer (1000 additional mappings) trained on a cleaned dataset
  • Char with clean dataset: Novel character-level tokenizer (similar to StyleTTS) trained on the same cleaned dataset
