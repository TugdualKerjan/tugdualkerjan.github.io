Exploring Barilla's Pasta Varieties

I want to eat all the pasta shapes proposed by Barilla.

Unfortunately, when contacting their customer service I failed to get a satisfactory answer concerning finding a comprehensive list of all their products.

I resorted to using web scrapping to obtain the full list of Barilla products sold on their website, and then, after having removed duplicates, sorted 1300 items using google sheets IMAGE() function, noting if it had a number referenced on it or not. You can find the google sheet here

After going through the list I transformed it into a JSON file that could be loaded and displayed thanks to Javascript and OpenLayers.

Below, you'll discover a map detailing the availability of specific pasta types across different countries.
