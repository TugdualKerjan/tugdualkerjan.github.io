# [INTERWEB NUGGETS](https://t.me/interwebnuggets)
![](www.png)

On the 31st of August, inspired by friends that had created channels on Telegram I decided to have a go myself on a subject that I enjoy: Fantastic, quirky websites found in the vast depths of internet. Across the years, any time I would fall on one of these I would simply post it there to grow my collection. Yesterday this channel reached a total of 1000 squirrels all collecting and sending these small nuggets of beauty through a bot. For me, this channel is my most precious example of an atomic habit that transforms a 5 second reflex into something great ! Have a go at it yourself, I'd love to be your first subscriber
