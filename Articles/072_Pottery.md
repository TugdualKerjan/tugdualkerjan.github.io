# POTTERY
![](pottery.png)

During a LAN at a friends place I noticed all their plates and bowls were homemade pottery - I asked and ended up taking a subscription with Jeanne to <a href="http://lempotee.ch/">L'empotée</a>, a wonderful little workshop in St-Sulpice. We've been going regularly for around 10 weeks now and have been slowly working up our skills on the wheel, working different types of ceramic glazes, and more complex shapes. By far the toughest skill to learn is patience with the wheel as the clay can easily break and get all wobbly. The satisfaction of using our own plates and bowls is amazing, and a wonderful show and tell for when friends are over, or as a gift ! I can only recommend this for anyone looking to spend 2 hours with friends chatting over some pottery making.
