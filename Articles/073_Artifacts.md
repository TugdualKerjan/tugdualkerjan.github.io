We are all products of our environment. For example, the biggest predictor of your weight are your friends. It'd be hard to be a computer scientist in an environment without computers, or speak French without having been born in a world with French speakers. The fish are the last to see the water and Yuval's <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/23692271-sapiens">Sapiens</a> has really made me think of the difference between norms, or `myths` as he calls them, and biology. Notably on sexuality, success and health. I'll be writing more about what changes I wish to bring to my environment to make sure as a monkey I'm adhering to principles that maximize what the young ones would call vibing. A first step is realizing what's influenced and made me _me_, so I've built a small website doing just that.
