# [PHYSICAL COMPUTING CLUB](https://physicalcomputing.club)
![](lilguy.png)

Computers aren't just electronic ! The first ones were actually mechanical, and our wet brains can also calculate outputs given a function and an input. There is a whole community working in different directions on this idea, and which meets up once a year to talk about this subject during the <a href="https://computingwithphysicalsystems.com">Computing with Physical Systems</a> conference which I had the occasion to go to this year. I want to build the community that is needed to exchange ideas in this space, thus I created the <a href="https://physicalcomputing.club">physicalcomputing.club</a> where researchers, students, industry and curious people can all come together to exchange their ideas and resources in this field !
