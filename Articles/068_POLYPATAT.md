# [POLYPATAT](https://tugdual.fr/potato.pdf)
![](Projects/Website/Articles/images/potat.png)

During my studies at EPFL we were required to take a class to expand our horizons outside of the bubble that is the geeky on the spectrum engineers that we learn to be - specifically, a class called "Penser la nature" where final project was to relate some natural object and it's interaction with man. A fantastic class with a lot of intriguing aspects, and we thus decided to talk about the potato with Kuba. Ensued a wild project of proposing to all students to come and chop up their potatoes and give them to us in exchange for fries (These chopped up potatoes would then serve as the next fries). Over 20kg of potatoes later, and clothes drenched in the wonderful smell of oil, PolyPatat was born. It's now going strong with a 4th edition just having been finished 🥔.
