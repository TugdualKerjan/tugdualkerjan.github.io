# [VROOM](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1fW2NSZ3Q_mfhTchGJd3PfJvHQ3obPxxc/view?usp=sharing)
![](Projects/Website/Articles/images/LOGO.png)

At the start of Febuary last year with 3 other friends we decided to launch a hitchhiking competition at EPFL - the idea is that all teams meet up at noon on Saturday and discover the destination address. They then have to get over there as fast as possible, carry their tents to sleep over there or anywhere if they haven't managed to get there by night time ! We spiced things up by offering a 150db horn to the team that brought the most obnoxious object with them - Microwaves, tires and drying stand where brought. People arrived driving a Tesla and others having made a short stop to milk cows in exchange for a ride. By far, this is my proudest achievement in terms of "Associatif" at this wonderful school. 3rd edition coming soon 👀
