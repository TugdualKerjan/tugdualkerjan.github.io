# PLANKING
![](planking.png)

10 minutes! After 4 months of planking every day until failure I managed to plank for 10m03s, having started with a PR of 1m30 🎉! This is a habit I plan on keeping for life, heavily inspired by the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ea0fUDES1Hc">following video</a>. So as to make sure the habit stuck I applied the same technique I had used when running for 100 days. Making my habit resistant to breaking on days I’m sick, tired: Planking one second was enough to check the box. The benefits I have seen are: Better posture, increased tolerance to discomfort and muscle gain, and becoming even more convinced that consistency is an amazing mechanism to doing great things!
