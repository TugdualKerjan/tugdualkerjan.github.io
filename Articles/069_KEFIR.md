# KIFIR
![](biere.png)

After spending 4 years with Jane I've had the pleasure of being accustomed with Kefir, a fizzy drink that is made from fermented organisms. I love it so much, in fact, that I wanted to distribute bottles of it to friends and family, and ideally even start a buisness out of it ! Thinking big but starting small, I bought a pack of cheap beer, emptied them and washed off the labels with Sodium Percarbonate and hot water. I also bought hobby brewing material and thus was able to capsulate my own bottles ! Finally, to put the cherry on top with friends we created bottle labels with nano-banana, google's image generator with realistic looking nutritional tables (Probably illegal lol). All in all it was a great success and I want to see if I can scale it up further 🔥
