# [RSS](https://tugdual.fr/rss.xml)
![](rss.png)

RSS is basically the proto-social network. It's a protocol that allows you to get content from various websites that you wish to follow and receive it in a dedicated application on your device. It allows you to "follow" people on internet that post content in whatever format, and simply also provide a document formatted in XML that will be sent to you whenever there is a new update. Without ads. Or algorithms. Just you and the people you want to hear about, without needing to remind yourself to open the website every now and then
