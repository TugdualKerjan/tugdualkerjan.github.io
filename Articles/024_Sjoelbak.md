# SJOELBAK
![](wood.png)

Back From Paques, an event organized by AGEPoly (Association for the students of EPFL) budgeted 150 CHF for me to build this famous dutch shuffleboard. Measurements were taken rapidly from <a href="http://www.van-vliet.org/dempseywoodworking/sjoelbak.shtml">this site</a>. The wood cost ~70 CHF and the varnish another 10. The little peices of wood were made out of truck flooring plywood and a CNC. A dark varnish allowed the material to look more authentic
