# PARAGLIDING
![](para.png)

After a wonderful week at UCPA, an outdoor sports non profit in France I was able to fly on my own for around 5 minutes over 450 meters in the air ! The 5 day program taught us everything from safety basics to how local and global weather patterns affect the wind direction; how one trades off altitude for speed; and the different steps to launch ourselves in the air autonomously. Half of the fun was also doing this with wonderful people from all places - ski instructors, medicine students, construction workers, a cop, a cook and a analog circuit designer for space ships all coming together to discover a new sport. The joy we shared after landing from our first autonomous flight was something beautiful, tears of happiness were shared ! A wonderful experience that definitely won't be the last!
